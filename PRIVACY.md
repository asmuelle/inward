# Inward — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 20 June 2026_

Inward is built on a single promise: your words never leave your phone.

## What we collect

Nothing. Inward does not collect, transmit, store off-device, or sell any
personal data. There is no account and no login.

## Where your data lives

Everything you create — voice recordings, transcripts, written entries, themes,
and weekly reflections — is stored **only on your device**, in an encrypted
database. Speech-to-text and the weekly reflection are produced on your device
using Apple's on-device frameworks. None of it is uploaded to us or to anyone
else. You can confirm this yourself: enable airplane mode and use the entire app,
then check Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ App Privacy Report.

## Network use

The only part of Inward that uses the network is in-app purchases, which are
handled by Apple through StoreKit. Apple processes your purchase under
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We never receive
your name, email, payment details, or any other personal information from that
process.

## Analytics and tracking

There are none. Inward contains no analytics services and no third-party
tracking software. The App Store privacy label for Inward is **Data Not
Collected**.

## Exporting your data

You can export an encrypted copy of your journal at any time. The export is
sealed with a passphrase that only you know; we never see it and cannot recover
it. Once you save or share that file, it is yours to manage.

## Children

Inward collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new
date above.
