Inward is a private place to think out loud.

Speak or write, and your words are transcribed and kept on your phone — and only your phone. There is no account, no cloud, and no server to breach. You can prove it: turn on airplane mode and use the whole app.

VOICE-FIRST JOURNALING
Talk, and Inward listens and writes it down, right on your device. Read it back, edit a line, keep it. When you would rather type, the written path works exactly the same.

GENTLE WEEKLY REFLECTIONS
Once a week, Inward gathers the themes that kept coming back — each one linked to the entries it came from, in your own words. Nothing is invented; every line points back to something you actually wrote.

YOURS ALONE
Lock Inward behind Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode. Your entries are encrypted on disk.

ENCRYPTED EXPORT
Save a copy of your journal anywhere you like, sealed with a passphrase only you hold. Reading your entries and exporting them are free, always.

WORKS OFFLINE, EVERYWHERE
On a plane, underground, or abroad with no signal — Inward never needs one. Everything happens on your phone.

Reading and exporting your words are free forever. After a 7-day free trial, a membership unlocks unlimited new writing and the weekly reflections.

No accounts. No servers. No tracking. Your words never leave this phone.
