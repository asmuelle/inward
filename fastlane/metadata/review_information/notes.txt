Inward is a private journaling and self-reflection app. Everything runs on the device.

How to verify the core promise:
1. Enable airplane mode.
2. Record or type an entry — capture, transcription, saving, and the weekly reflection all work with no connection.
3. Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ App Privacy Report shows no network activity from Inward.

There is no account and no login, so no demo credentials are needed.

In-app purchases (auto-renewable membership and a one-time lifetime option) unlock new writing and the weekly reflection after a 7-day free trial. Reading existing entries and exporting them are never gated.

All inference (speech-to-text and the weekly reflection) is performed on device with Apple frameworks. The only network the app uses is StoreKit for purchases. No analytics or tracking is present; the App Privacy label is "Data Not Collected."
